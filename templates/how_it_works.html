The Anti-Gaming Layer

Why recruiters trust a CredScore more than a resume. We've built robust systems to prevent fake reviews and manipulated scores.

🛡️

Blind Mutual Ratings

Neither the student nor the business can see the other's rating until both are submitted, preventing retaliatory scores.

⏱️

Timestamp Verification

Our system tracks the exact time of assignment and submission. You can't fake punctuality or 'speed-run' complex tasks.

🤖

Anomaly Detection

Algorithms flag suspicious patterns like the same business repeatedly rating the same student 5 stars within days.
