About CredScore.

CredScore was built on a simple premise: college students do amazing work every day, but have no verifiable way to show it to employers.

We are bridging the gap between academia and industry by turning real-world tasks into a verified performance metric. By doing so, we help small businesses access affordable talent while giving students the platform to build their careers before they even graduate.
